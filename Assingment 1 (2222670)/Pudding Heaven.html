Egg Pudding

Egg pudding is a silky, smooth, steamed custard dessert made from sugar, eggs, and milk. It's found in many Asian cuisines, particularly Japanese purin with caramel sauce and Chinese steamed egg pudding. Our egg pudding recipe will show you how to make this traditional dessert at home.
